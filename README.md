### And here are a few ideas that turned into things I'm proud of 
- **ECON STUFF**
  - **[Insurer Competition in the Age of Provider Consolidation](aca_competition_consolidation/paper/Insurance Competition in the Age of Provider Consolidation.pdf):** Wow that's a mouthful. Started as an undergraduate thesis completed with the help of my wonderful advisor Professor Colleen Grogan; I've spent the past year making some substantial updates that I think make the work much more compelling. Using public data from CMS, and private data from the American Hospital Association (AHA), I tried to measure the impact of reduced healthcare provider competition on competition and prices in ACA exchanges. In doing so, I contributed to the existing literature by (1) illustrating how "rating areas" offer researchers a powerful tool for model specification when studying ACA exchanges, and (2) outlining a novel approach to hospital market HHI construction using hospital "radii", instead of generalizing results across counties using rating area boundaries as past work has done.
